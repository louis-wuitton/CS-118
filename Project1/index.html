Welcome to the WaterCom testbed, managed by the UCLA Network Research Lab. WaterCom allows users from all over the world to run under-water networking experiments. From here you may run a sample experiment consisting of two nodes transmitting data to eachother using different parameters, or for more in-depth experiments using more nodes and multi-hop networks, you may sign up for an ssh account from the sign-up page.
